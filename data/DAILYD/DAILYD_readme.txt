DailyDialog (Dialogue Corpus)

Total number of dialogues: 13117
Average number of turns: 102968
Chanel Corpus ID: DAILYD

Description: A high-quality multi-turn dialog dataset, DailyDialog, which is intriguing in several aspects. The language is human-written and less noisy. The dialogues in the dataset reflect our daily communication way and cover various topics about our daily life. We also manually label the developed dataset with communication intention and emotion information. Then, we evaluate existing approaches on DailyDialog dataset and hope it benefit the research field of dialog systems.

Reference: The original paper on DailyDialog is available at the arXiv anthology https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.03957. Yanran Li and Hui Su and Xiaoyu Shen and Wenjie Li and Ziqiang Cao and Shuzi Niu, (2017) "DailyDialog: A Manually Labelled Multi-turn Dialogue Dataset", in CoRR abs/1710.03957.
